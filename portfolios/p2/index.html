Our Story

Born from a passion
for the perfect cup.

Lumière began in 2019 as a weekend pop-up at the local farmers' market. Two baristas, one hand-built roaster, and a dream to share coffee the way it was meant to be experienced.

Today we source directly from family farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra — building relationships that span seasons, not just transactions.

  • Direct trade with 8 family farms across 4 countries
  • All beans roasted fresh weekly in-house
  • Baristas trained with Specialty Coffee Association certification
  • Compostable packaging & zero-waste kitchen
